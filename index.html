Pitch

An Action game with Tower Defense elements where you defend the Protective Crystals of Teryndor! As Prince Caelorn, fight against hordes of creatures to save your kingdom from destruction.


Synopsis

The kingdom of Teryndor is protected by the Guardian Crystals—sacred relics that emanate the blessing of the Goddess Vaelith, the Earth Goddess. According to legend, Vaelith sealed away a primordial entity of chaos and infused her essence into the crystals to maintain the world’s balance.


As long as the crystals remain intact, Teryndor thrives: harvests are abundant, the creatures of the forests remain calm, and the borders are secure. However, someone or something is now targeting the crystals, unleashing chaos upon the kingdom.


King Draegor’s army occupied defending the northern, western, and southern borders, the already-neglected Eastern region is left undefended. When a peasant arrives at the throne hall pleading for help, the king admits he has no soldiers to spare.


Driven by duty, Prince Caelorn sets out alone for the East.

Features

- Explore 3 unique regions, each with 7 challenging stages.

- Face hordes of creatures with unique behaviors that require different tactics to defeat.

- Prove your worth in battles against legendary bosses.

- Unlock and master 9 distinct weapons, each with 3 power levels. Evolve your arsenal to combat ever-increasing threats.

- Test your skills in the 3 endless stages and compete for a place on the global leaderboard to become Teryndor's greatest defender.
